The Registration to the Conference is mandatory to be enabled to attend the Sessions. Participation to the conference is free of charge. To this end it is mandatory to complete the registration process by September 10th, 2022. Registration includes all conference sessions, including the Plenary Session, technical workshops, user presentations, and special interest group meetings. In addition, select content from the in-person event will be available after the conference closes. Details and registration for digital access will be announced in the coming weeks. Registration for the in-person conference incudes access to digital content.
